classic pasta salad:

a perfect for a buffet or a barbecue.Customize with your favorite vegetable.Ripe olives,italian dressing,and Chddar cheese combine for a forkful of tangy,satisfying flavor.

ingredients:

  • 8 ounces rotelle or spiral pasta,cooked and drained
  • 2 ½ cups assorted cup-up vegetable(broccoli,carrots,tomatoes,bell peppers,cauliflower,onions and mushrooms)
  • ½ cup cubed Chaddar or mozzarella cheese
  • ⅓ cup slice pitted ripe olives(Optional)
  • 1 cup Wish-Bone italian Dressing

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients except Wish-Bone® Italian Dressing in large bowl.
  2. Add Dressing; toss well.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
